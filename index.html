About Sol

Sol Choi Reiter is 30 years old, born in Seoul, South Korea and is currently living in St. Louis, Missouri. Before getting married in 2012 in Las Vegas, her korean name was 죄은솔, which translates to Choi, Eun-Sol. Choi is her family name and Korean's last names are based off their families historical clan.

Korean siblings names are often similar and made from two syllables. Her sister's first naem is Eun-Byeol. Sol (short for Eunsol) is physically far from her family currently, but stays close through facetime video chat talking with her parents and sister. Her dad owns a commercial AC repair company.

Being a military spouse for 7 years can be tough at times. With the military comes a lot of travel and relocation. Sol makes the best of the situation by learning new languages such as Spanish and making new friends everywhere she goes.

Sol has two dogs which she loves dearly! Jinx is a 4 year old German shepard and Lilly is a 2 year old Mini Schnauzer. Shes active on Social media platforms such as Instagram and Twitter. The dogs seem to be popular and have over 10,000 followers in Korea!
